Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a one-page website?

Most one-page websites can be completed in 3-5 business days from the time I receive all your content and information. For more complex projects, it may take up to 7 days.

Do I need to provide all the content?

Yes, you'll need to provide the text content and any specific images you want to use. However, I can help with basic content editing and can source stock photos if needed. I'll guide you through exactly what's needed during our initial consultation.

Can I update the website myself after it's built?

Yes! I'll provide you with simple instructions on how to make basic updates to your site. For more complex changes, I offer affordable maintenance packages or can handle one-off updates as needed.

Will my website work on mobile phones?

Absolutely! All websites I build are fully responsive and mobile-friendly. They'll look great and function perfectly on smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktop computers.

Do you offer website hosting?

Yes, I can recommend affordable hosting options and help you set everything up. I'll ensure your domain is properly connected and your site is secure with an SSL certificate.
